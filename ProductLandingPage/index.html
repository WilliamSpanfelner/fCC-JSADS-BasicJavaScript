Track your drive's progress

Drive Doctor provides status updates regarding your drive's progress from the moment it arrives.

Timeframes and Actions

Drive Doctor will attempt to recover your data within 30 days of receipt.

Throughout the process Drive Doctor provides status updates regarding your drive's progress from the moment it arrives to the moment it departs our premises and finally arrives in your possession.

A successful recovery means that your data will be copied to a new encrypted storage device and returned to you. If by change the recovery attempt is unsuccessful, Drive Doctor will destroy your drive securely and in an environmentally friendly manner or return it via mail upon request.
